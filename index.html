About

NYC based fullstack software engineer interested in design, accessibility, and health.

I have expertise in fullstack web development and building applications from the ground up. My focus is on fast, responsive, and clean frontend design with a reliable and secure backend.

Graduate from App Academy, NYC's top rated programming bootcamp. The program consisted of a 16-week course with 80-100 hours of programming a week. In these 16 weeks I not only gained the ability to develop applications from scratch, I've also grown my knowledge in data structures and algorithms, giving me the ability to design high efficiency applications.

Can't code all day, so I enjoy fitness and health. I'm very invested in politics and current events. And when I need to just relax, you can catch me doing outdoor activities like hiking or listening to some good music.
